Flagship

The Berat Tour

7–10 hours Hotel pick-up Max 4 guests

Meet your guide at your hotel and transfer to Berat, with a pleasant stop at the beautiful Monastery of Ardenica. Explore the old quarter of the Castle of Berat and one of the oldest churches in the Balkans; discover the Onufri Museum inside the old Cathedral. Learn the city's history, admire the peculiar architecture of the old churches, and hear the beautiful — and slightly eerie — legend of the city's foundation. Walk the picturesque alleys of the Mangalem and Gorica quarters, and finish with a cold drink and rare, unparalleled panoramic views.

€210 · per private group · no prepayment
Flagship
Half Day · from Durrës & Golem

The Tirana Tour

2.5–3.5 hours Hotel pick-up Max 4 guests

Meet your guide at your hotel and enjoy a comprehensive tour of Tirana's most important landmarks — the City Center, the Castle of Tirana, The Pyramid, The Block, The Palace of Congresses, Mother Teresa Square, and the Lake Park. A fully tailored experience, adapted throughout to your specific interests and pace.

€135 · per private group · no prepayment
Half Day · from Tirana, Durrës & Golem

The Kruja Tour

3.5–5 hours Hotel pick-up Max 4 guests

Meet your guide at your hotel and visit one of the most historically significant cities in Albania — a small nation with a heavy footprint on medieval European history. Explore the National Museum of Gjergj Kastriot Skanderbeg, Albania's national hero, and learn how this land halted the advance of the era's most frightful empire. Wander the authentic 16th-century bazaar and discover one of Europe's most fascinating religious traditions.

€170 · per private group · no prepayment
Short Tour · from Golem & Durrës

The Durrës Tour

1.5–2.5 hours Hotel pick-up Max 4 guests

Meet your guide at your hotel or the centre of the city. Explore the history of one of the oldest and most important cities in the Balkans, visit the Archaeological Museum and the Amphitheatre, and walk along the impressive Byzantine walls of old Durrës.

€110 · per private group · no prepayment
